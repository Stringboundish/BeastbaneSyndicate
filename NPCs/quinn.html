Backstory

Quinn gave up glory when a Skyborn Roc destroyed his village. He survived only because a hawk shielded him as a child. He took the name Hawkstring to honor that bond. Now, Quinn acts as scout and sharpshooter for the Beastbane Syndicate, accompanied by his raptor companion Valor — an intelligent hawk with eyes like stormglass and a screech that can freeze a man mid-run. Personality: Calm, observant, focused. He speaks little, but his eyes miss nothing. Quirks: Sleeps perched in trees. Taps his bow before each shot. Motto: “Above, always.”
